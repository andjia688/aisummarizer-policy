Privacy Policy for AI Summarizer

Effective Date: September 20, 2025

Information We Collect

AI Summarizer processes text that you highlight or input for summarization. We do not collect personally identifiable information. Text may be sent securely to third-party APIs (such as OpenAI) for processing.

How We Use Information

Text is used only to provide summaries. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers. Any temporary data is discarded after processing.

Data Storage

We do not permanently store your input data. The Extension does not create user accounts or track browsing history.

Third-Party Services

Summaries may be generated using OpenAI’s API. You should review OpenAI’s Privacy Policy for more details.

Your Choices

You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the Extension. If you have concerns, contact us at: youremail@example.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
